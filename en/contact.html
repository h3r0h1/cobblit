Frequently Asked Questions

What are the main advantages of the Cobblit? add remove
The Cobblit offers unparalleled structural integrity thanks to its minimalistic, high-quality design. It eliminates unnecessary connections, resulting in a cleaner final result and faster installation, fully in line with our vision of technical elegance.
How does the Cobblit work? add remove
The system utilizes a patented slide-and-lock mechanism. By sliding the precision-milled components together, a seamless connection is created that secures itself without visible screws or bolts.
What material is the Cobblit made of? add remove
Each unit is manufactured from anodized aerospace-grade aluminum and reinforced with glass-fiber-enriched polymer. This ensures maximum strength with minimum weight and a premium industrial finish.
Is the connector easy to assemble? add remove
Yes. The design is explicitly focused on intuitive assembly. No special tools are required for standard applications, and the included minimalistic guide leads you step-by-step through the process.
Is Cobblit suitable for hard-to-reach places? add remove
Thanks to its compact and reduced profile, the system excels in tight spaces. Where traditional connections often require significant workspace, our system can be anchored almost blindly and in limited angles.
Can the connector be used in combination with a pedestal? add remove
Absolutely. The underside is equipped with universal mounting points compatible with most standard industrial pedestals, ensuring a solid and aesthetically pleasing base.
